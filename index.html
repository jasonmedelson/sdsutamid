About us

What is Tamid?

​The TAMID Group is a student-led, non-profit organization 501(c)3 , which develops and fosters the future relationship between American business-minded students and the Israeli economy. Founded in 2008, TAMID is thriving at nearly thirty elite U.S. universities. TAMID affords students an unprecedented opportunity for hands-on, daily interaction with Israel’s economic landscape.

Benefits

Network

Tamid Group consists of over 1500 college students and is rapidly expanding. Interact with students at 35 Universities across the country.

Education

AMembers participate in an interactive educational program that integrates business leadership principles with Israel’s economic landscape.

Fellowship

Members are given the oppurtunity to travel to Isreal for an eight week Internship.

Consulting

Members may choose to join student consulting teams, advising Israeli companies on the solutions to important business problems.

Investment

Members can also choose to join the Investment Fund program, in which teams conduct equity research and manage a stock portfolio.

Life after college

Tamid group continues to provide oppurtunities to engage past graduation.
